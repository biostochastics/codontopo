=== [Optimality landscape literature] ===
ANSWER: The genetic code evolved through selection, history, and chemistry; Knight, Freeland, and Landweber's 2001 review highlights its evolvability and redundancy. The code's evolution remains largely unexplained, with theories including coevolution and error minimization. The code's near-universality suggests strong selective pressures.
